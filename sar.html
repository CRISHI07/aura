Shipping & Returns

1. Shipping Policy

We offer worldwide shipping with estimated delivery times varying based on your location.

2. Processing Time

Orders are processed within 2-3 business days. You will receive a tracking number once shipped.

3. Shipping Costs

Shipping fees vary based on the destination and order size. Free shipping is available for orders over $50.

4. Returns Policy

You can return products within 7 days if unused and in their original packaging.

5. Refunds

Once your return is approved, refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days.

6. Exchange Policy

Exchanges are available for defective or damaged products. Contact us at support@aura.com.

7. Contact Information

For any shipping or return inquiries, reach out to our customer service team at support@aura.com.
